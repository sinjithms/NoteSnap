NoteSnap Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to NoteSnap!

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the NoteSnap extension.

Information We Collect

We don't collect any personal information from users through the NoteSnap extension.

However, NoteSnap may collect non-personally identifiable information such as browser type, language preference, and usage data for analytics purposes to improve the extension.

How We Use Your Information

We may use the non-personally identifiable information collected to analyze how the extension is used, troubleshoot issues, and improve user experience.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
