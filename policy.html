NoteSnap Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to NoteSnap!

This Privacy Policy explains how NoteSnap handles your personal information when you use our extension.

Data Collection and Use

NoteSnap does not collect or use any personal information from users. We adhere to the following principles:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect any personal information from users through the NoteSnap extension.
  • No Sharing of Data: Your data is not sold to third parties or used for any purpose outside of the approved use cases.
  • No Unrelated Uses: Your data is not used or transferred for purposes that are unrelated to the NoteSnap extension's core functionality.
  • No Use for Creditworthiness: Your data is not used or transferred to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
